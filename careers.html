Come Work with Us

We believe in using technology to address the absolute greatest challenges in the world. In the past year, we've already begun to experience extraordinary progress towards drastically improving the way global health is approached, and completely reworking the distribution of life-saving resources.

Macro-level Impact

We build software for governments, building the tools decision makers use to affect 100+ million person populations. We work at the highest levels of government, redefining how aid gets delivered to millions of people suffering from preventable and curable disease.

Core Ownership

There’s tremendous potential for growth, and we’re committed to help you get there. At Zenysis, you’ll take meaningful ownership of key products from week one, and see them through to completion.

Immense Demand for your Work

In under a year, our product has been piloted in Ethiopia and South Africa and funded by major global health organizations such as the Gates Foundation. We've also raised $2.8M from VCs including Founders Fund, The Omidyar Network, Y Combinator and 500 Startups.
